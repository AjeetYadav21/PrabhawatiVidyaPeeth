About Prabhawati Vidyapeeth

Building a Strong Foundation for Academic Excellence and Character Development

Our Vision & Mission

Prabhawati Vidyapeeth is a premier educational institution located in Sahatwar, Ballia, Uttar Pradesh, dedicated to providing quality education to students from Nursery to Grade 12. Affiliated with the State Board, we offer a comprehensive curriculum that nurtures academic excellence and character development from early childhood through senior secondary education.

Our school stands out with its unique multilingual approach, offering instruction in Hindi, English, Sanskrit, and Urdu, ensuring students develop strong linguistic skills alongside their core academic competencies.

We believe in holistic education that goes beyond textbooks, fostering critical thinking, creativity, and ethical values that prepare students for success in an ever-changing world.

Academic Programs

Comprehensive Education from Nursery to Grade 12

Nursery & KG

Early childhood education with play-based learning, developing foundational skills in a nurturing environment that encourages curiosity and creativity.

Primary School (1-5)

Building strong academic fundamentals with engaging teaching methods, focusing on literacy, numeracy, and holistic personality development.

Middle School (6-8)

Strong foundational curriculum focusing on core subjects including Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Languages. Building critical thinking and study skills.

High School (9-10)

Comprehensive State Board curriculum preparing students for board examinations with focus on conceptual understanding and practical application.

Advanced studies with stream options preparing students for higher education and competitive examinations with personalized guidance.

Admission Process

Step 1: Inquiry

Contact the school office or fill out the online inquiry form to get detailed information about admissions.

Step 2: Application

Obtain and complete the admission form with all required information and documents.

Step 3: Assessment

Attend the scheduled assessment or interview as per grade requirements.

Step 4: Confirmation

Upon selection, complete the admission formalities and fee payment to secure your seat.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (Original & Copy)
  • Previous School Transfer Certificate
  • Academic Records/Mark Sheets
  • Address Proof
  • Parent/Guardian ID Proof
  • Passport Size Photographs
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
